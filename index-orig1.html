How it works

Anonymously Ask A Black Person (ABP) gives you access to the brilliant minds of Black People. Ask us any question. Send us a message and a black person will answer you.

Example Questions

  • Do you prefer to be called Black or African-American?
  • Who are your top 5 rappers?
  • Why are there so few blacks in tech?
  • Why do you love TV Shows like Scandal & Empire?
  • Do all black people like chicken & watermelon?

Frequently Asked Questions

What can I use ABP for?

For asking any questions to a black person.

How do I know if it's a black person answering me?

Trust us, it's the internet

Can I really ask anything?

Yes, but ... If you send us something racist, crazy, illegal, you are held accountable.

How much does this cost?

Luckly for you its free! Except your normal text message phone charges and limits. That's on you!

Can I pay you for your answers?

Sure! Email us and we'll send you payment information.

What if I don't get an answer?

Try again, Black people have to work too.
